About AeroSense

AeroSense is a cutting-edge platform dedicated to monitoring and detecting the health status of birds. By combining advanced AI algorithms with real-time analytics, AeroSense allows bird enthusiasts, researchers, and wildlife conservationists to accurately assess the well-being of their feathered companions. Our mission is to simplify bird conservation and make health monitoring accessible for everyone. We provide insights into activity patterns, feeding behaviors, and environmental influences, helping users intervene early in case of health issues. Through alerts, analytics, and interactive dashboards, AeroSense empowers proactive care and supports research on avian health trends. By fostering collaboration between hobbyists, researchers, and conservationists, AeroSense strengthens efforts to protect bird populations and preserve biodiversity. From domestic pets to wild species, our platform ensures healthier birds and safer habitats, contributing to a more sustainable and informed approach to avian conservation.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does AeroSense monitor bird health?

It uses AI-driven image analysis and behavioral tracking to detect health issues early.

2. Can I track multiple birds at once?

Yes, you can add multiple birds and monitor their individual health profiles.

3. How do I get alerts?

Alerts can be sent via email or in-app notifications based on configured thresholds.

4. Can I export the data?

Yes, historical data and analytics reports can be downloaded in CSV or PDF format.

5. Is my data secure?

AeroSense uses encrypted storage and secure servers to protect your bird data.
